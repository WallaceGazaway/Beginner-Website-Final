Surface Cities


Ecosystem of the Sector

With resources so limited, and with very little space to live on and cultivate, people are incentivized to give away any children they have beyond the bare minimum. The children given away to Orphanages are treated as tools and resources, stripped of their humanity by having their arms and legs replaced for limbs well suited to exploring the deep recesses below, while also making them less appealing to potential parents who might use the facility for less vile means.
