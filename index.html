The Hollowing Pit

What is the Hollowing Pit? It is a world falling into an unexplainable and seemingly irreversible state of decay, like a rotting fruit showing the pit inside. With little space for everyone to live, the people have taken to desperately forcing swathes of children into labor, going far below to find resources and, maybe, answers.
