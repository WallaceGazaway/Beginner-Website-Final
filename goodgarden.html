Ecosystem of the Sector

The creatures dwelling in the pit are of a very peculiar sort. They appear nowhere else above-ground, only in the pit itself. The lifeforms of the uppermost sector seem to be the tamest of their ilk. Despite their strange appearances, they still largely follow the logic of the average animal, such as with the deer-like creatures at the top of the forest's foodchain. With the body of a deer, and a head like a cross between owl and moth, this creature camoglauges with its green-down covered body, and it scares its opposition with fake eyes. The whale-like mouth on their face has lead the children who found it to call it the Smile Stag.
